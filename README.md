CleanSpace Privacy Policy
Effective date: April 23, 2026

CleanSpace (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how CleanSpace handles information when you use our iOS/iPadOS application.

1. Information We Access
CleanSpace may request access to:

Photos (to scan duplicate/similar photos, screenshots, large videos, and Live Photos for cleanup features)
Contacts (to detect and merge duplicate contacts)

2. How We Use Information
We use accessed data only to provide app features, including:

Photo and video analysis for storage cleanup suggestions
Contact duplicate detection and merge operations
Displaying local cleanup history and storage estimates on your device

3. Data Collection and Sharing
We do not require account registration.
We do not sell your data.
We do not share personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing.
We do not transmit your photos or contacts to our servers for processing.
Processing is intended to occur locally on your device.

4. Permissions
You can choose whether to grant permissions:

If Photos or Contacts permission is denied, related features will not work.
You can change permissions anytime in iOS/iPadOS Settings.

5. Data Storage and Retention
Cleanup results/history used by the app are stored locally on your device.
When you delete photos/videos via the app, deletion is performed through Apple’s Photos framework. Items may remain recoverable in Apple’s “Recently Deleted” album based on system behavior.

6. Children’s Privacy
CleanSpace is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Security
We rely on Apple platform security and app sandbox protections to safeguard data accessed by the app.

8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a revised effective date.
